LTISD Facility Use Agreement
Please read the agreement, then check the box to acknowledge.

Preface

Lake Travis ISD Board Policies GKD (Legal) and GKD (Local) address Non-School Use of School Facilities. The District shall permit non-school use of designated facilities for educational, recreational, civic, or social activities when these do not conflict with school use or applicable laws, policies, or rules.

Application Requirements

Applications must be filed no less than 21 days prior to the requested date(s) and no more than 90 days in advance. Not officially booked until signed by the Community Relations Liaison or Director/Assistant Director of Athletics.

Scheduling Priorities

District educational programs and extracurricular activities always have priority. The Community Relations Liaison or Athletic Director may cancel a scheduled non-school use if an unexpected conflict arises.

Rental Costs & Payment

Invoices issued monthly after completed events. No deposit required. Payments mailed to: Lake Travis High School — ATTN: Athletics, 3324 Ranch Road 620 South, Austin, TX 78738. Cancellations within 24 hours incur the full facilitator fee. 6% penalty for payments 30 days past due + 15% administrative/support services penalty.

Conditions of Use

Use only leased area · Do not exceed capacity · No transfer to third parties · Representative present at all times · No equipment storage · No pets without approval · No smoking, drugs, or alcohol · No open flames or firearms · Comply with all parking rules and applicable laws.

Insurance Requirements

Commercial General Liability: $1M per occurrence / $1M aggregate. LTISD listed as Additional Insured. Auto Liability: $500K CSL if vehicles on property. Workers' Comp at statutory limits if employees on property.

Indemnity

Lessee agrees to indemnify and hold harmless Lake Travis ISD, its agents, trustees, officers, and employees from any and all claims arising out of or connected with the use of LTISD facilities.
